EQ and IQ

What is Emotional Intelligence(EQ)?

Emotional intelligence is all about understanding thoughts and feelings, but isn't in tune to just your own but others and how to process those thoughts and feelings when interacting with others

How is it different to Intelligence(IQ)?

EQ is the emotional part of intelligence which is understanding and reading your own thoughts and feelings and others. IQ is about ones technical capability and usually decides academic skills

Why is it important?

Learning about EQ is very important for us all because it leads to better understanding others and creating better relationships with ourselves. It also helps us to grow as people.
